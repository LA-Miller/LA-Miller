Hello, I'm Luke Miller.


I'm a Full-Stack Web-Developer.

My passion for programming began at the beginning of the pandemic. I have always been captivated by video games and I originally began coding thinking that I wanted to become a game developer. I dove into python, c#, and c++ and got some knowledge of how basic syntax works by making some simple video games. Later on, I found out about Eleven Fifty Academy and that's when my passion for web development started. Web development gives me the satisfaction of solving difficult problems, while also giving me an artistic outlet. I have a decent grasp on html, css, and javascript, and can solve most problems with the help of some outside resources.

Besides coding, my other hobbies include backpacking, video games, and exercising. I plan on completing a thru hike of either the Appalachian Trail, or the Te Araroa trail in New Zealand.
